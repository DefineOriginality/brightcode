FAQ

Common questions

Do kids need any prior experience?

Not at all. CodeSprouts and our introductory tracks are designed for absolute beginners. If your child can use a tablet or a mouse, they're ready.

Do families need to supply a computer?

Yes. Students will need access to a computer or tablet for our online sessions. Personal computers are strongly preferred for the best learning experience, as our website is optimized for desktop use and may not be available on mobile or district-regulated devices.

Are programs really free?

Yes. Our core programs are 100% free thanks to our donors and partners. For private lessons, a small donation is required. The amount varies based on the complexity of the instruction, the topic, and the time involved.

Are all classes online?

Yes, all courses are conducted online via Zoom to ensure accessibility and flexibility for our students. No in-person classes are offered.
