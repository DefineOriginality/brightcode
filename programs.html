All Programs

Find the right fit

From playful Scratch projects for 6-year-olds to full-stack apps for teenagers — we've got you covered.

CodeSprouts

Ages 6–9

The perfect introduction to programming. Students use Scratch's visual drag-and-drop interface to create animated stories, games, and interactive art — learning logic and sequencing without writing a single line of text code.

Scratch Sequencing Loops Events Creative Projects
📅 10 weeks 👥 12 max per class 💰 Free
🌿

CodeExplorers

Ages 10–13

The bridge from visual to text-based coding. Students are introduced to Python fundamentals — variables, conditionals, and loops — by building small games, calculators, and command-line programs they can actually use.

Python Variables Functions Conditionals Mini-Projects
📅 12 weeks 👥 12 max per class 💰 Free
🚀

CodeBuilders

Ages 14–17

For teens ready to build real things. Students learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create responsive websites, then explore Python further with data projects and APIs. The program culminates in a portfolio-worthy capstone project.

HTML & CSS JavaScript Python APIs Portfolio Project
📅 16 weeks 👥 10 max per class 💰 Free
☀️

Summer Intensive

Ages 8–16

An immersive two-week summer camp experience. Each day is packed with coding challenges, team projects, and guest talks from industry professionals. Students leave with a completed project and new friends who share their passion.

Immersive Team Projects Guest Speakers Hackathon
📅 2 weeks 👥 20 max 💰 Subsidized
💜

Girls in Tech

Ages 10–16

A safe, empowering space designed specifically for girls and non-binary students. This cohort-based program pairs coding education with mentorship from women in the tech industry, addressing the gender gap head-on.

Python Web Design Mentorship Leadership
📅 12 weeks 👥 12 max per cohort 💰 Free
🏫

School Partnership

Ages 6–17

We bring BrightCode directly into partner schools as an after-school club or elective. Our instructors handle equipment, curriculum, and facilitation — schools just provide the space. Fully funded for Title I schools.

On-site All Levels Fully Funded Flexible Schedule

FAQ

Common questions

Do kids need any prior experience?

Not at all. CodeSprouts and our introductory tracks are designed for absolute beginners. If your child can use a tablet or a mouse, they're ready.

Do families need to supply a computer?

No. We provide laptops or Chromebooks for all in-person sessions. For online programs, we offer a device lending program.

Are programs really free?

Yes. Our core programs are 100% free thanks to our donors and partners. Summer Intensive has a subsidized fee; no one is turned away for an inability to pay.

How do I enroll my child?

Fill out the enrollment form on our Contact page. We'll reach out with availability and placement based on your child's age and experience level.
