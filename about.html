Our Story

We believe every child is a future builder

Founded in 2019, CodeKids grew from a single after-school class into a movement reaching thousands of children across our region.

Mission & Vision

Breaking down barriers to tech education

CodeKids was born from a simple idea: no child should be left behind in the digital age. Our founder, a former software engineer and educator, noticed that the kids who needed technology education most were the ones who had the least access to it.

Today, we partner with schools, community centers, and libraries to deliver free and subsidized coding programs that meet kids where they are — in their neighborhoods, in languages they speak, at the level they're at.

✊
Equity First

We prioritize underserved communities, offering 100% free programs to qualifying students.

🎯
Project-Based Learning

Students learn by building — real games, apps, and websites they're proud to show off.

💡
Curiosity Over Perfection

We celebrate mistakes as learning opportunities. There is no "wrong" answer when you're exploring.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

These principles guide how we teach, how we hire, and how we grow.

🌱

Inclusion

We design every program to welcome all backgrounds, abilities, and learning styles. Diversity makes our community stronger.

🔥

Passion

Our instructors aren't just teachers — they're builders who love what they do and pass that enthusiasm on to every student.

📈

Growth Mindset

We teach kids that intelligence is built, not born — and that every error in your code is just a step toward a working solution.

🔍

Transparency

As a nonprofit, we publish our annual impact reports and financials. You can always see exactly where every dollar goes.

❤️

Care

We see the whole child, not just a student. Safe, encouraging, supportive spaces are the foundation of real learning.

🤝

Community

Programming is more fun together. Collaboration is baked into every project, because real-world tech is always a team effort.
