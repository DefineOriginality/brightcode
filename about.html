Our Story

We believe every child is a future builder

Founded in 2019, BrightCode grew from a single after-school class into a movement reaching thousands of children across our region.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

These principles guide how we teach, how we hire, and how we grow.

🌱

Inclusion

We design every program to welcome all backgrounds, abilities, and learning styles. Diversity makes our community stronger.

🔥

Passion

Our instructors aren't just teachers — they're builders who love what they do and pass that enthusiasm on to every student.

📈

Growth Mindset

We teach kids that intelligence is built, not born — and that every error in your code is just a step toward a working solution.

🔍

Transparency

As a nonprofit, we publish our annual impact reports and financials. You can always see exactly where every dollar goes.

❤️

Care

We see the whole child, not just a student. Safe, encouraging, supportive spaces are the foundation of real learning.

🤝

Community

Programming is more fun together. Collaboration is baked into every project, because real-world tech is always a team effort.
