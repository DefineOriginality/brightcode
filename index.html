Why It Matters

Coding is the literacy of the future

We believe that access to STEM education should never depend on a zip code. Here's why what we do is critical.

🧠

Problem Solving

Coding teaches structured thinking, logic, and creative problem-solving skills that apply to every area of life.

💼

Career Readiness

Tech jobs are the fastest growing sector. Early exposure gives underserved kids a real path to high-paying careers.

🤝

Community Impact

When one child learns to build technology, they carry that knowledge back to their families and neighborhoods.

Programs

Something for every young learner

Age-appropriate, project-based programs that make learning to code exciting and rewarding.

🌱

CodeSprouts

Visual block-based coding using Scratch. Kids build animations, games, and stories — no prior experience needed.

Ages 6–9
🌿

CodeExplorers

Introduction to Python and web basics. Students create real-world small projects and start thinking like developers.

Ages 10–13
🚀

CodeBuilders

Advanced HTML/CSS/JS and Python projects. Teens ship real apps and build portfolios for college and career.

Ages 14–17
